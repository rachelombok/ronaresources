Professional Grants

  • Freelancers Relief Fund - Assistance of up to $1,000 per household for any independent worker in the U.S. who has primarily earned income through freelance work for at least one year an dhas experienced a recent, sudden, and unforeseen hardship or loss of income as a direct result of the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • A Better Balance Help - A free helpline (call or email) that offers answers to questions about the law for workers facing issues at work related to sick, family, or medical leave.
  • Emergency Coronavirus Tipped & Service Worker Fund - Cash assistance to restaurant workers, care service drivers, deliver workers, personal service workers.
  • MIT Open Courseware - Thousands of free MIT courses online, from humanities to STEM.
